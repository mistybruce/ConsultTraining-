World Famous Blue Crab Festival

Blue Crab Festival

The Blue Crab Festival is a festival held annually in Little River, SC. The festival attractions thousands of visitors and locals alike. Food, entertainment and vendors are in abundance! The festival is held on the waterfront and spans two full days of festivities!

The Strawberry Festival

The Strawberry Festival

The Strawberry Festival is a yearly celebration held each spring in Chadbourn, NC. People from all over Columbus county get involved in the festival. The strawberry festival includes a parade, a pageant, lots of entertainment for the entire family and of course, tons of strawberries! The Strawberry Festival is alwaysbig fun in a small town!

The Carolina Country Music Festival

The Carolina Country Music Festival

The Carolina Country Music Festival is held in Myrtle Beach, SC. This is one of the areas newest festivals, however it is proving to be very popular. The shows sell out rapidly and include major headlining performers. This annual festival is sure to grow as the years pass and is already becoming a favorite in the area!
